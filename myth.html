The Myth of the Speaker's Circle

Long ago, language flowed like rivers through the valleys of every land but when the people lost their voices, 7 great stars rose into the sky to guard the voices of the Earth. Each star contains a 1000 tongues—spoken, sung, and now forgotten.

“The Speaker’s Circle Constellation shines not just with light, but with memory. Stand beneath them, and you may hear the whisper of a lullaby from far away. The stars form a sacred circle in the night sky, a constellation visible to all people of Earth regardless of their language or culture.”

Children were chosen to become Keepers-Guardians of the Starseeds. By listening, learning, and playing beneath the stars, they return fragments of language to the world and ensure the ancient wisdom is never truly lost.
